In this game, you will be given a small paragraph of random letters. Your task is to count the number of times a single letter occurs in the paragraph. You are given a total of 12 similar rounds in separate pages, and the time allotted is 5 minutes for all the rounds combined. If this task is chosen for payoff, you will receive 1 experimental token for each correct answer, each worth $1.00 for each correct answer. Each trial has an equal chance of being selected.
